ANAADYANTA 2016!!!

An inter collegiate annual techno cultural fest started in the year 2004 has attracted the attention of many colleges. Anaadyanta in 2011 was inaugurated by the chief guest Mr. Shashidhara Kote,Kannada classical and Janapada song singer. The number of colleges and the number of students participating in this event are increasing year after year. This techno cultural festival includes a variety of items like Fashion show, Dance, music, debate, Quiz, sports events, essay writing, Badboyz Toys,Battle of Bands, Cad/Cam, Collage, Computer Gaming, Dumb Charade,Fox hunt,Matarazzi,Mehendi Design, Rangoli, madads,Yakshagana etc. Mr. Sri Murali, kannada cine star was the chief guest for the valedictory function

Anaadyanta, a Sanskrit word meaning the one which has neither the beginning nor the end. Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology hosts its inter-collegiate techno-cultural fest under the banner of "Anaadyanta" Prizes worth Rs. 2.5 lakh!!!

Events: Battle of Bands(electric),Battle of Bands(Acoustics),Computer Gaming,NFS,Counter Strike,DOTA,Fashion Show,Photography,Mad Ads,Mock Rock,JAM,Air Crash,Beat Boxing and Rap,General Quiz,Stress Buster,Treasure Hunt,Tug of War,Su-do-ku,Debate,Free lance,Make me a Model,On spot Painting,Dumb Charades,Collage,Stenography,Flameless Cooking,Mehendi design, Hog-a-thon,Rangoli,Antakshari,Vocal Solo,Vocal Group,Solo Dance,Indian Group Dance,Western Group Dance,T 20,Mini Soccer,Slow Bike Racing,Jugglers,Volley Ball,Bad Boys Toys (bikes, cars)
